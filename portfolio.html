I may also be known as PoiKoi!

Is the brand and online identity I adopted from 2016. The name was developed from my interest of Anime at the time, as well as my admiration to the beauty and variety of the Koi. The logo was develop in a sketch book, it encorporates the name within the body of the koi.

The logo was later redeveloped in 2022 into the single colour salmon-pink background. The colour change was implemented in order to fit a modern and clean aesthetic, more inline with other companies and brands. The previous colours clashed with each other, so the more pastel pink/red was adopted.

VTuber Model

With the modernisation of technology, online entertainment such as livestreams or videos, has branched out and developed various methods for creators and platforms to more easily create content. VTubers (Virtual Youtubers) is a persona or character created by content creators to appeal beyond the traditional camera stream content. VTubers can be a 2D or 3D character, but both characters use similar tracking systems to map the facial, or even body movement of a person.

In this project, I designed, drew and rigged the 2D VTuber model. The project was aimed to express and utilise my skills in artistic software and programming. I taught myself about the modelling software Live2D, and was also granted the oppourtunity to trial with AI generation software during the design ideation phase of this project.
